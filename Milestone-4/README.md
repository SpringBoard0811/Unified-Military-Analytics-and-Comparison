🤝 Milestone 4 – Coalition Builder Dashboard
Unified Military Analytics and Comparison Dashboard
Milestone 4 introduces the most advanced analytical module of the project — the Coalition Builder dashboard.

This dashboard allows users to simulate alliances by selecting multiple countries and analyzing their combined military strength, KPIs, and budget impact compared to individual nations.

🎯 Objective of Milestone 4
This milestone focuses on:

Simulating multi-country military alliances
Aggregating military metrics dynamically
Comparing coalition power with individual countries
Analyzing how defense budget and manpower scale in alliances

Coalition Builder.pdf
📊 Dashboard Features
🌍 Country Selection
Multi-country slicer to build a coalition All visuals update based on selected countries

🪖 Total Active Personnel
Displays combined manpower of the coalition

✈️🚢🛡 Total Power (Air, Land, Naval)
Shows aggregated military assets across land, air, and sea

💰 Defense Budget per Country
Visual of budget contribution from each country in the coalition

💵 Defense Budget per Soldier
Indicates spending efficiency of the coalition

🌐 Location on Globe
Map showing geographic spread of coalition countries

📊 Coalition Budget Percentage
Shows coalition’s share of global defense spending

🧠 KPIs Used
Power Index Rank Gap Assets per Capita Defense Budget-to-GDP Ratio

🔄 Workflow
Select Countries → Aggregate Metrics → Recalculate KPIs → Visual Comparison

✅ Outcome
Interactive alliance simulation Aggregated military and budget insights Coalition vs country level comparison
