# 🌍 Unified Military Analytics & Comparison Dashboard (2025)

> **An Advanced Defense Intelligence Analytics Platform**
> Transforming global military data into actionable strategic insights through interactive, cross‑platform dashboards.

---

## 🎯 Project Objective

To build a **full‑scale Military Intelligence Analytics System** that enables users to:

* ✔ Analyze global military strength rankings
* ✔ Perform country‑level strategic capability assessment
* ✔ Compare military power between nations
* ✔ Simulate alliance and coalition strength
* ✔ Generate data‑driven geopolitical insights

---

## 🌐 Why This Project Matters

Global defense capability analysis is critical for:

* 🌍 Geopolitical intelligence and research
* 🛡 Strategic defense planning
* 📊 Policy analysis and think‑tank studies
* 🤝 Alliance capability evaluation

This platform converts **fragmented open‑source defense data** into a
**unified, intelligence‑ready analytics ecosystem**.

---

## 🛰 Data Source

**Primary Source:** Open‑source defense statistics (scraped)

**Collection Method:** Automated Python scraping pipeline using a predefined URL list

**Dataset Coverage:**

* 🌎 140+ countries
* 📈 50+ military & economic indicators

**Example Metrics:**

* Military manpower
* Aircraft inventory
* Naval assets
* Armoured vehicles
* Defense budgets
* Logistics capability
* Economic indicators

## 📊 Analytics Modules

### ⚡ Global Command Center

A high‑level global military intelligence dashboard.

**Highlights**

* Worldwide Power Index Rankings
* Top Military Nations Leaderboard
* Region & Alliance Filters
* Strategic KPI Cards

---

### 🌍 Nation Intelligence Profile

Deep‑dive military analysis for any selected country.

**Insights Covered**

* Manpower & Force Readiness
* Air Power & Aircraft Fleet
* Naval Combat Strength
* Land Systems & Armoured Units
* Defense Budget Allocation
* Derived Strategic KPIs

---

### ⚔️ Power Comparison Lab

Side‑by‑side comparison of two countries.

**Compare Across**

* Personnel Strength
* Aircraft Inventory
* Naval Assets
* Defense Spending
* Composite Power Metrics

---

### 🤝 Coalition Strategy Simulator

Simulates combined military strength of allied nations.

**Capabilities**

* Multi‑country alliance builder

* Coalition vs Single Nation comparison
* Strategic power modeling
